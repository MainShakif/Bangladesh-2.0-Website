Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of turning new ideas or methods into something valuable, like improved products, services, or ways of doing things.

Sustainability

Sustainability is meeting present needs without compromising future generations, balancing environmental protection, social equity.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the fundamental physical and organizational structures, systems, and facilities like roads, power grids etc.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the degree of autonomy and self-determination in people and in communities. This enables them to represent their interests.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.
